## Description (≤ 4000 chars)

Keptt turns household chores into a game the whole family wants to play.

Create tasks, assign them to family members, earn points for every completion, and redeem those points for real rewards — all from a beautifully simple app that just works.

**KEEP YOUR HOUSEHOLD ON TRACK**
• Create daily, weekly, or monthly chores with custom emojis
• Each task earns points — set higher points for harder jobs
• Tasks reset automatically after completion, so nothing falls through the cracks
• A daily reminder badge tells you exactly how many tasks are overdue

**MAKE CHORES A FAMILY ACTIVITY**
• Invite your partner, spouse, or adult family members by sharing a 6-character code
• Add child accounts (no email needed) so the whole family joins in
• A live leaderboard tracks weekly points so kids can see where they stand
• Medals go to the top three earners each week 🥇🥈🥉

**BUILD STREAKS & EARN REWARDS**
• Watch your completion streak grow day by day
• Set up custom rewards — movie night, extra screen time, a special treat — and let family members spend their earned points
• Rewards show a "Can redeem" badge the moment a member has enough points

**DESIGNED FOR FAMILIES**
• Clean, distraction-free design with large tap targets (great for kids)
• Works offline — data syncs instantly when connectivity returns
• No ads, no subscriptions, no third-party tracking

**NOTIFICATION SUPPORT**
• Optional daily push notification at 8 AM listing today's open tasks
• Badge count on the app icon shows overdue tasks at a glance
• Tap the notification to go directly to today's task list

Keptt is free to download and use. Your household data is stored securely and is never sold or shared.
